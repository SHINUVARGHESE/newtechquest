Overview

TechQuest Innovations, we are proud to offer our innovative Estimator tool to our clients, providing them with a comprehensive solution to plan and budget their healthcare projects accurately. Our Estimator tool is intended to support our clients throughout the initial phases of project development, enabling them to decide on the viability and cost of their project with knowledge.


Why are we the best?

Our Estimator tool is built on advanced algorithms and methodologies, ensuring that it provides reliable and accurate estimates based on the project scope, location, and other critical parameters. By identifying possible cost reductions and empowering users to make well-informed choices about project design and scope, our technology saves clients both time and money.

Why choose us?

At TechQuest Innovations, we have over 25 years of experience in the healthcare consulting industry, providing turnkey solutions for healthcare projects. Our team of experts includes well-experienced professionals in healthcare facility planning and design, MEP engineering, medical equipment planning, and healthcare project management. We are committed to delivering projects within the agreed timeline and budget, ensuring that our clients' expectations are exceeded.

Features and Benefits

Our Estimator product comes with several features that make it an indispensable tool for healthcare facilities:

Accurate Cost Estimates: The Estimator's primary feature is its ability to provide accurate cost estimates for healthcare construction projects. With the Estimator, healthcare facilities can input project details, such as location, size, and required equipment, and receive an accurate estimate of the project's total cost. This function is crucial because it gives healthcare facilities a clear idea of the expenditures involved in their construction project, enabling them to plan and budget.

Customizable Parameters: The Estimator allows healthcare facilities to customize several parameters to reflect the unique characteristics of their project. These parameters include location, square footage, equipment, and additional requirements. By customizing these parameters, healthcare facilities can receive a more accurate cost estimate that reflects their unique project requirements.

User-Friendly Interface: The Estimator has a user-friendly interface that allows healthcare facilities to input project details and receive an accurate cost estimate quickly. No of their degree of technological expertise, all healthcare professionals can use the interface because it is simple to use and intuitive.

Cost Savings:The Estimator can help healthcare facilities save money by providing an accurate cost estimate. By having a clear understanding of the project's cost, healthcare facilities can identify areas where they can save money, such as by using different materials or adjusting the project scope. Save money and stay within budget by using budget-friendly methods.

Implementation and Support: TechQuest, we offer implementation and support services for our Estimator product. We work closely with healthcare facilities to ensure that they can implement the Estimator seamlessly into their workflow. In order to ensure that healthcare professionals can utilize the Estimator to its fullest capacity, our team of specialists is available to offer training and assistance.
