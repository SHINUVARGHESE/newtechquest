Innovative Healthcare Infrastructure

In order to satisfy the changing demands of their patients and staff, healthcare organizations must adjust their infrastructure as they expand and change. TechQuest has created a modular device to streamline what may be a difficult and time-consuming procedure.

Our modular product offers a flexible and adaptable solution for healthcare facilities looking to expand or upgrade their existing infrastructure. It is a prefabricated building system that enables the quick construction of facilities with unique designs. It enables quick and effective adjustments to match changing needs thanks to customisable modules that may be added or withdrawn.

Soft Modular:

Our soft modular product is a versatile and flexible solution for healthcare facilities looking to expand or change their existing infrastructure. With customizable panels and components, it allows for quick and efficient modifications to meet changing needs. This solution is ideal for areas such as patient rooms, operating rooms, and emergency departments.

Hard Modular:

Our Hard Modular product is a more durable solution for healthcare facilities that need a more permanent solution. This solution is ideal for areas such as clinics, laboratories, and pharmacies. It offers the same level of flexibility as our Soft Modular product but is constructed from more durable materials to withstand daily wear and tear.

Whole Room Modular:

Our whole-room modular solution is perfect for healthcare facilities looking to expand or construct new facilities. It enables the modular creation of full rooms, including the walls, ceiling, fixtures, and fittings. This solution is ideal for areas such as operating rooms, patient rooms, and emergency departments.

Customizable Modules

TechQuest, we understand that every healthcare organization has unique needs and requirements. That's why our modular product is customizable. Each client receives individualized attention from our team of professionals as they create a modular structure to suit their demands.

Our modular product offers a wide range of customizable features, including:

Room layout: Our modular buildings can be designed to meet specific room layouts, whether it be for a surgical suite, an imaging center, or a pharmacy.

Materials: We provide a variety of materials for our modular buildings, including more friendly materials like bamboo as well as conventional building materials like concrete and steel.

Finishes: Our modular buildings can be finished with a wide range of materials, including tile, carpet, and vinyl. We also provide a variety of external construction finishes, such as metal, brick, and stucco.

Integration of technology: High-speed internet, video conferencing, and electronic medical records can all be integrated into our modular buildings.

The Benefits of Modular Construction

Modular construction has become popular in recent years due to its many advantages. One of the main advantages is how quickly construction is completed.

Because the modules are prefabricated in a factory, construction time is reduced. This means that healthcare organizations can get their new facilities up and running much more than with traditional construction methods.

The degree of customisation that is available with modular construction is another advantage.

Modular buildings can be designed to meet specific requirements, whether it be for a surgical suite, an imaging center, or a pharmacy. This means that healthcare organizations can create the ideal environment for their patients and staff, tailored to their unique needs.

Modular construction is also cost-effective. Because the modules are prefabricated in a factory, there is less waste and fewer delays due to weather conditions or other external factors. This means that construction costs are often lower than with traditional construction methods.

The flexibility of modular construction also allows for future expansion. Healthcare organizations can add new modules to their existing facilities as their needs grow and evolve. This implies that the initial financial commitment made to modular building will likely continue to pay dividends for many years to come.

The modular construction process

TechQuest, we pride ourselves on our efficient and streamlined modular construction process. The following steps make up our process:

Consultation: Each client's specific demands and requirements are understood by our team of professionals through collaboration with them.

Design: Based on the client's needs, our team creates a custom design for the modular building.

Prefabrication: Once the design is finalized, the modules are prefabricated in our factory.

Site preparation: While the modules are being prefabricated, the site is prepared for installation. This includes site grading, foundation preparation, and utility connections.

Installation: The prefabricated modules are transported to the site and installed on the foundation.

Finishing: Once the modules are installed, the building is finished with flooring, fixtures, and other finishes.

Inspection: To make sure that the building satisfies our exacting standards of quality, our team does a complete inspection of it.
