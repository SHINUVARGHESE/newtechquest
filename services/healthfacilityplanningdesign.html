What is Health Facility Planning & Design?

The process of creating and planning healthcare buildings, such as hospitals, clinics, nursing homes, and other facilities related to healthcare, To improve processes and patient outcomes, the goal is to provide a comfortable and secure atmosphere for both patients and employees.

This involves careful consideration of factors such as space utilization, equipment placement, lighting, and ventilation.
