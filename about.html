WE ARE TECHQUEST

Konstruct is an construction company, proudly serving since 1991. We specialize in New Building Construction, Tenant Improvements, Interior & Exterior Renovations, Building Expansions.

Capability

TAHPI is one of the most prolific authors of International Standards and Guidelines for Healthcare Design customised for different regions of the world. TAHPI is the largest developer of software and web-based tools for healthcare Service Planning, Functional Briefing, Supply Capture, Mapping and Equipment Planning for the entire healthcare industry.

A to Z of Health Planning

Healthcare is a critical and highly specialised discipline that should only be entrusted to the most experienced organisations. TAHPI is a natural choice for the total delivery of healthcare solutions as our area of expertise is healthcare only. Our wealth of experience and proven systems ensure that we deliver on time and budget as demonstrated by our impressive track record.

Methodology

TAHPI has developed its delivery methods over many years such that we can deliver more efficiently with the minimum of stress. Our methodology utilises several important stages involved in delivery of healthcare projects.

Awards

Award certificate for Premier’s NSW Export Awards 2011 in Health Planning Australian National Export Awards 2011 in Health Planning Service Worldwide Hospital Build Award 2012 in Dubai “The Best Initiative to Improve the Design Standard of Healthcare Facilities”.

Healthcare Technology

Healthcare is becoming more complex in a challenging environment where hospitals are expected to provide more services, faster, and budgets are under pressure. Medical Technology is viewed as a solution to improving productivity and control costs while maintaining the highest quality care.
