Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


What is TechQuest Innovations Pvt. Ltd.?

TechQuest Innovations Pvt. Ltd. is a project management consultancy company that provides comprehensive solutions for healthcare industry projects. Our services include concept designing, space planning, architectural works, MEP design, equipment planning, and project management.

How long has TechQuest Innovations Pvt. Ltd. been in business?

TechQuest Innovations Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2019, however, our team has more than 25 years of combined experience in the healthcare sector.

What is a turnkey project?

A project that is completely designed, constructed, and prepared for usage is referred to as turnkey. TechQuest Innovations provides turnkey project solutions for our clients in the healthcare industry.

What is project management?

Planning, coordinating, and managing resources to accomplish a project on schedule, within budget, and to the appropriate quality standards is the process of project management. TechQuest Innovations provides comprehensive project management solutions for healthcare industry projects.

What is concept design?

The process of developing a design concept that complies with the demands of the client is known as concept design. TechQuest Innovations provides concept design services for healthcare industry projects.

What is space planning?

The practice of maximizing a facility's utilization of space is called space planning. TechQuest Innovations Pvt. Ltd., provides space planning services for healthcare industry projects.

What is MEP design?

MEP design refers to the design of mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems in a facility. TechQuest Innovations provide MEP design services for healthcare industry projects.

What is equipment planning?

Equipment planning is the process of ensuring that a facility is equipped with the latest healthcare technologies and best practices. TechQuest Innovations provide equipment planning services for healthcare industry projects.

How do I contact TechQuest Innovations?

You can contact us through our website or appointment schedule with our team.

What is the ROI guarantee provided by TechQuest Innovations?

TechQuest Innovations Pvt. Ltd., we assure a return on investment in every square foot of space with professionally and functionally designed projects. We ensure that our client's facilities are optimized for maximum efficiency and functionality, ensuring that they receive the maximum return on investment.
